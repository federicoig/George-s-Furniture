    About George's Furniture

    Hi, I'm George! and I've been in this bussiness for 20 years. All started when my father, John, set himself into the furniture bussines 65 years ago, he inculcated to me and my siblings the principles of working hard, saving money and be competitive enough to stand out. After my father passed away, I opened my own and new store in Freehold, New Jersey. Started to expand through the world. By now there's 243 George's Furniture stores around the USA, and 498 in another countries like the United Kingdom, Germany, Italy and France. If you want to know more about me, you may come to visit me at my store and discover the high quality of my furniture!
